  1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

    2. Specifies the URL where form data is sent.

  2. form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

    3. method: Specifies how the form data is sent (GET or POST).

  3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

    4. GET: Sends data in the URL, visible, for retrieving data.
    POST: Sends data in the request body, hidden, for submitting or modifying data.
